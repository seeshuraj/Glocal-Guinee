Our Journey from 2008 to Today

GLOCAL GUINEE SARLU has been a cornerstone of Guinea's export sector since 2008. We pride ourselves on ethical sourcing and premium quality control.

  • 2008: Founded in Conakry, starting with Sesame exports.
  • 2015: Expanded to Raw Cashew Nuts (RCN) with shells.
  • 2022: Reached new heights in Cocoa distribution to Asian markets.
